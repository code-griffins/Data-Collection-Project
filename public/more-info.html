The Kenya Medical Practitioners, Pharmacists and Dentists Union (KMPDU), the Linda Ugatuzi Civil Society, medics and a number of civil society organisations have asked the government to return management of the national health system from the County Government to the National Government. The County Governments lack enough resources to cater for the professionals, staff and support-staff in various hospitals across the county. 7 million signatures are required to ammend the constitution for the establishment of a Health Services Commission; which will provide a centralised mechanism for addressing issues affecting public health; such as the shortage of health professionals, drugs among others. Join the movement for a better change since devolution has weakened health care delivery in the country in the last 10 years of devolution.
